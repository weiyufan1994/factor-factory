# Step 4 contract

## Purpose
Step 4 executes a generated factor on the shared clean daily layer and emits standard evaluation evidence for Step 5/6. It must not rely on ad hoc screenshots, notebook-only plots, or one-off temporary scripts as official evidence.

## Inputs
- `factor_spec_master__{report_id}.json`
- `data_prep_master__{report_id}.json`
- `handoff_to_step4__{report_id}.json`
- runnable factor implementation from Step 3B
- shared clean daily data prepared by the authorized data steward workflow

## Mandatory outputs
- `factor_run_master__{report_id}.json`
- `factor_run_diagnostics__{report_id}.json`
- `handoff_to_step5__{report_id}.json`
- backend `evaluation_payload.json` files under `factorforge/evaluations/{report_id}/`
- `runs/{report_id}/factor_values__{report_id}.parquet` or `.csv`
- `runs/{report_id}/run_metadata__{report_id}.json`

## Mandatory self-quant evidence pack
`self_quant_analyzer` must emit all standard artifacts below before Step 4 can be treated as complete:

- `rank_ic_timeseries.png`
- `pearson_ic_timeseries.png`
- `coverage_by_day.png`
- `quantile_returns_10groups.csv`
- `quantile_nav_10groups.csv`
- `quantile_counts_10groups.csv`
- `quantile_summary_table.csv`
- `long_short_returns_10groups.csv`
- `long_short_nav_10groups.csv`
- `quantile_nav_10groups.png`
- `quantile_counts_10groups.png`
- `long_short_nav_10groups.png`

## Metric contract
`self_quant_analyzer` must include `standard_metric_contract` in its payload. Blocking checks include missing artifacts, non-finite IC metrics, malformed decile return/NAV tables, empty decile groups, non-positive NAV, and NAV not normalized to 1.0 at the first observation.

## Plot discipline
Manual or temporary plotting is not official Step 4 evidence. If a plot/table is needed, it must be added to the backend artifact contract, generated by `run_step4.py`, and validated by `validate_step4.py`.

## Reproducibility warning
Tiny fixtures may still exist for smoke testing, but official research cases require the full standard evidence pack above.
